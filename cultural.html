Culture & Family

I am of Chinese ethnicity, although I was born and raised in New Zealand. Originally both my parents lived in China before coming to New Zealand for university. At home, I am mostly immersed in the Chinese culture, such as eating Chinese foods, celebrating holidays/festivals and speaking mainly Mandarin. The rest of my family still resides in China, which we visit every year.
